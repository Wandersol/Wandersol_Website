Our Mission

We Power People to Empower People

Our mission is to maximize the experience of those who wander by providing them with comfortable, trustworthy and durable gear that will satisfy their desire to explore what the world has to offer. However, not only do we define our success by serving our customers wants and needs, but by helping others along the way. For each backpack purchased, Wandersol will donate a backpack to a person experiencing homelessness. We believe that we can give power, to empower those who need it most.
