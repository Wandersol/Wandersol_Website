The WanderSol Pack is a rugged, futuristic, and eco-friendly backpack that harnesses the power of the sun. The built-in panel, when fully charged, can power your phone, a small radio, a portable light or even a small personal-sized fan.

The straps are made of a durable nylon-polyester blend material that will keep your contents safe and dry. They are ergonomically made for comfort and ease of adjustment. The solar panel itself fits inside an outer pocket-shield that you can remove from to place in the sun to charge. It will also charge inside the shield. The panel features an LED light display (?) that will indicate the amount of charge left as well as a 5V port that is accessible via a USB adapter cable.

The inside of the WanderSol Pack has two compartments. One for larger items like a laptop or camera, and another for smaller items that you may want to keep separate and find easily. The interior is lined with …… and is also waterproof.

Designed for equal parts outdoor enthusiast and on the move commuter, the WanderSol Pack is ready to power you on through your next day's outing, whether it’s to the mountains or to the office!
